WHY

PEOPLE BUY FROM PEOPLE - EVEN MORE SO IN LUXURY

MyLuxee is an easy web-to-store solution that maintains the relationships between the retail luxury stores and their clients.
The platform creates close links allowing customers to get in touch with theirs favorite stores thanks to privileged contacts with salespeople.
MyLuxee is a new shopping experience, borned out of two basic admissions in the luxury retail industry.

For luxury shoppers, there is nothing better than dealing with sales associates they know well. Regular clients even call them by their first names.
Abroad or in places they know less, they often prefer being understood and advised by those who speak their own language.
Brands hire specialists, fluent, but these are not always at full disposition due to different timetables.

WHY NOT being aware of associates' availability and contacting them straight ?
Who better than salespeople can advise you personally, book a good or even reserve some time for you ?
Let stores' salespeople become your luxees !
